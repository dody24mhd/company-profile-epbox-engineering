PLC Programming & Integration

PLC (Programmable Logic Controller) is the backbone of industrial automation. Our team specializes in:

  • Custom PLC programming for various brands (Siemens, Allen-Bradley, Omron, Schneider, etc)
  • Integration with sensors, actuators, and field devices
  • Process automation, logic design, and troubleshooting
  • Remote monitoring and diagnostics

We ensure reliable, scalable, and future-ready PLC solutions for your industrial needs.

SCADA Systems

SCADA (Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition) enables real-time monitoring and control of industrial processes. Our SCADA services include:

  • SCADA system architecture design and implementation
  • Integration with PLCs, HMIs, and field devices
  • Custom dashboards, alarms, and reporting
  • Secure remote access and data logging

We deliver robust, user-friendly SCADA solutions for efficient plant management and decision making.

Control Panel Solutions

We design and build high-quality control panels for a wide range of industrial applications:

  • Custom-built panels for automation, process control, and safety
  • UL/CE/IEC compliant panel design and documentation
  • Panel wiring, layout, and component selection
  • Testing, certification, and on-site commissioning

Our panels are engineered for reliability, safety, and long-term performance in demanding environments.
